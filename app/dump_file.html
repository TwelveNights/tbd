Inspiring possibilities

Work or play, the Dell Inspiron 15 has the power and speed to get the job done. An AMD A12-9700P processor, 8GB of memory, and huge 1TB hard drive are great for heavy-duty multitasking, while a 15.6-inch Full HD touchscreen delivers a razor-sharp view for movies, games, and everything in between. There's also a backlit keyboard to keep you typing when the lights dim, and a precision touchpad for a more intuitive way to navigate your PC.

Brilliant Full HD TrueLife display

Dell TrueLife displays deliver darker blacks and colors that pop for vivid graphics and video, perfect for games, movies, and photos.

Speedy AMD A12 processor

The AMD A12-9700P delivers performance on par with Intel Core i5 and provides excellent multitasking and 4K Ultra HD support for next-gen gaming.

Discrete AMD Radeon graphics

Perfect for online gaming, AMD Radeon R7 graphics ensure games and multimedia run smoother, while advanced power management ensures efficient system performance.

Studio-quality sound

Whether you're mixing or streaming, Waves MaxxAudio Pro delivers immersive sound full of clear highs, enhanced bass, and increased volume.

Built to last

Inspiron laptops can survive short-term heat conditions up to 149F. Hinges are still tight after opening and closing the lid 20,000 times. Commonly used keys have been tested up to 10 million times.

The best Windows yet

Windows 10 delivers faster start-ups, a familiar yet expanded Start menu, and great new ways to get stuff done across multiple devices.
